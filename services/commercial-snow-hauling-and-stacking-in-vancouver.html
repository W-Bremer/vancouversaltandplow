Commercial Snow Hauling and Stacking in Vancouver

Plowing clears snow—but it doesn’t make it disappear.

When your lot runs out of space, snow piles become a liability. They shrink your usable area, create blind spots, and quickly turn into solid blocks of ice that are dangerous and expensive to deal with later.

Our hauling and stacking service solves that before it happens. Vancouver Snow Plowing moves snow fast, smart, and safely—keeping your site clear, your operations running, and your insurance risk low.

What the Service Covers

We offer two options:

  • Snow Stacking – Relocating snow to pre-approved zones on your property using loaders.
  • Snow Hauling – Removing accumulated snow off-site entirely, using dump trucks and licensed disposal sites.

Both are handled by our trained crews with the right equipment, operating on your schedule.

Why This Matters Now

Snow piles grow fast. What’s manageable during the first storm becomes a serious obstruction after the third. Parking spaces vanish. Drivers can’t see around corners. Runoff turns to ice sheets. That’s when complaints start—and slip-and-fall claims follow.

Avoid that chaos by planning snow relocation in advance. When a big storm hits, hauling resources city-wide are already stretched thin. If you're not booked, you're buried.

Who Uses This Service

  • Shopping centers with tight circulation space
  • Condo properties with underground parking or minimal surface area
  • Industrial yards with constant loading/unloading
  • Any commercial site that can’t afford to lose a stall to snow storage

How It Works

  • We assess your snow plan and layout ahead of time
  • Snow is relocated during or after storms using loaders and trucks
  • Sites are logged, photo-documented, and confirmed post-service
  • Emergency call-outs are available for existing clients when space becomes critical

Included in This Service

  • Loader and truck deployment
  • On-site stacking or full off-site hauling
  • Snow dump site coordination
  • Site check-in/out with visual logs
  • Optional pre-storm hauling prep

Snow Is Predictable. Pileups Are Preventable.

Every winter, we watch properties scramble to find hauling help too late. Don’t let your lot get buried.

Let’s talk now about hauling options—before you run out of space.
